# 🌿 Angel Creations

**Creating with aroha. Inspiring through AI.**

Angel Creations is the central creative headquarters for children's storytelling, educational resources, AI-assisted creative systems, natural product development, and business building.

## Mission

Create beautiful, practical, meaningful resources for children, whānau, educators, and communities while treating te ao Māori, wellbeing, and neurodiversity with care and respect.
